About GK Plumbing

Established in 2012 by Mr. Kaelo Motshotlwane, GK Plumbing is a Small Medium Enterprise Construction Company and division of Gopolang Katlego 09 Trading CC. We operate throughout Johannesburg and other provinces in South Africa.

Our mission is to build long-term relationships based on integrity, performance, and value for money. We specialize in small and medium-scale construction projects, property maintenance, repair, and alteration work.
